NOTE: GPU metrics collection requirements.

If your cluster contains GPU-enabled nodes, ensure the following for GPU metrics to appear in the DevZero dashboard:
1. DCGM (Datacenter GPU Manager) exporter is installed and running
2. DCGM exporter pods are annotated with prometheus.io/scrape: true

To verify GPU nodes in your cluster:
  $ kubectl describe nodes | grep "nvidia.com/gpu"

To check DCGM exporter status:
  $ kubectl get daemonset -A | grep dcgm

For detailed installation and configuration instructions, refer to:
  https://www.devzero.io/docs/k8s-automation/getting-started/step-by-step#nvidia-gpus-on-k8s
